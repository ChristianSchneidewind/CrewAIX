# Tweet Types for FlugNinja

These tweet types define the content strategy and tone of voice.
Overall tone: modern, friendly, professional, inspiring — not cringe, not salesy.

---

## marketing

Goal:
Promote FlugNinja and its services in a confident, benefit-focused way without sounding like ads.

Style:
- Professional
- Clear
- Value-oriented
- Not pushy

Angles:
- Problem → Lösung (klarer Pain, klare Hilfe)
- Myth-Busting (kurzer Irrtum + Korrektur)
- Social Proof (ohne fake Zahlen, ohne Übertreibung)
- Speed/Convenience (Zeit sparen, Aufwand reduzieren)
- Cost/Risk (kein Risiko, kein Vorab-Stress, transparente Schritte)

Rules:
- No emojis
- Max 240 characters
- Focus on benefits, not features
- No hard selling language

---

## educational

Goal:
Teach something interesting about flying, paragliding, or the experience.

Style:
- Informative
- Friendly
- Easy to understand

Angles:
- Mini-Guide (3 Schritte, ultra kurz)
- Begriff erklärt (1 Begriff, 1 Satz Erklärung, 1 Nutzen)
- Häufiger Fehler (Fehler → besserer Weg)
- Checkliste (kurz, praktisch)
- Fun Fact (1 überraschender Punkt)

Rules:
- May use 1 emoji
- Should feel like a fun fact or tip
- No marketing language
